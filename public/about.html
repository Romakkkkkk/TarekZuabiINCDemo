About Us

At Tarek Zuabi INC., we believe renting a car should be as smooth, reliable, and straightforward as the drive itself. Founded by Tarek Zuabi in Heidelberg, our company has grown from a local passion project into a trusted name for personal and business car rentals across the region.

Rooted in Heidelberg’s culture of precision and innovation, we combine German efficiency with a strong commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you need a compact vehicle for city travel, a luxury car for special occasions, or a practical fleet for business solutions, we tailor every rental to fit your journey.

What sets us apart is our dedication to trust, transparency, and quality service. From flexible rental plans to a fleet of well-maintained vehicles, we make sure every customer enjoys both peace of mind and freedom on the road.

At Tarek Zuabi INC., we don’t just rent cars—we help you move with confidence.
